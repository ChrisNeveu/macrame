trait All[Enum] extends NumericConverters[Enum] with OrderedModular[Enum]

This traits provides *all* of the auto-generated functions in EnumApi. It is rare that one actually needs all such functions and it is recommended that you use a smaller subset of these functions as provided by the other traits in the macrame.enumeration namespace.
